SHIVA

THE DESTROYER

Lord Shiva, one of the principal deities in Hinduism, is known as the destroyer or transformer within the Trimurti, the Hindu trinity that includes Brahma (the creator) and Vishnu (the preserver). Shiva is often depicted with a third eye, a trident (trishul), a crescent moon on his head, and a snake around his neck. He is associated with asceticism, meditation, yoga, and divine energy. Shiva is also revered as the patron god of yoga, arts,and fertility.
